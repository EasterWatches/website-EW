About Us

EASTER WATCHES is the flagship brand of STANDARD WATCH COMPANY, one of the country's leading manufactures / assemblers based in Bengaluru, Karnataka. Nearing 25 years of success in the field, EASTER brand watches enjoy a well-named reputation for unmatched quality, professional service, and exclusive varieties and ranges.

Under the brand name EASTER WATCHES, STANDARD WATCH COMPANY offers stunning ranges of watches for both men and women.

  • Gents / Ladies Leather Strap Watches
  • Gents / Ladies GPMS (Gold Plated Metal Strap) Watches
  • Gents / Ladies Bracelet Watches
  • Ladies Chicklet Watches

The range also includes Date and Day/Date Watches. A sampling of EASTER watches is provided in this catalog, but these are only the tip of the iceberg. We have more and more varieties to offer.

Easter watches perfectly suit your wrist requirements, taste, and budget.

We also have a unique system of After Sales Service, managed by the dealer. We provide full liberty to perform the required servicing for your watch or you can contact / approach the company using the details given below.

Additionally, we offer unique collections of Corporate Gifts/Complement ranges that will easily fit your budget and taste.
